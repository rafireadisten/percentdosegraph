PercentDoseGraph

Clinical Dosing Visualization Tool

🚀 In Development - Deployment Coming Soon

A lightweight browser app for clinicians and pharmacists to review medication dosing as a percentage of patient-specific maximum dose over different time windows.

Core Features

  • Patient & medication configuration
  • Dose event tracking
  • % of max dosing visualization
  • Multi-window analysis (1 day to 10 years)
  • JSON/CSV data export & import
  • 35+ medication reference library
